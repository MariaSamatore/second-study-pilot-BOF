Instructions
  • You will now play our game, Space Oddity. There are three levels to play. Each level takes 3 minutes. You need to survive the 3 minutes to proceed to the next level. If you lose midway, you are requested to restart that level. You will finish playing the game when you survive all three levels.
  • Play with sound on! There are sound effects that we want you to hear while you play.
  • The game can take some time to load depending on the machine you play on. Please be patient with the loading process.
