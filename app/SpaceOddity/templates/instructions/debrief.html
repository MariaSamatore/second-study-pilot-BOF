Debrief

When you played the game, you were randomly assigned to one of two groups to play a version of Space Oddity with or without features that potentially increase self-efficacy such as a progress bar, encouraging phrases, and displaying usernames.

Our goals are:

  • To determine if self-efficacy supporting features increase players' self-efficacy in Space Oddity, video games, and life.
  • To determine if self-efficacy supporting features affect Player Experience in regards to enjoyment and self-determination.
